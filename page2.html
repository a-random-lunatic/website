Choclate Chip Cookies

History

This recipe makes simple choclate chip cookies. These choclate chip cookies are chewy. Make sure to let them cool once you take them out of the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. preheat oven 375 degrees.put parchment paper on a baking pan and put it to the side.
  2. In a another bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. put to side.
  3. Mix together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Stir in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add 12 oz package of chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 Tablespoons of dough at a time into balls and put them on pan
  8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes.
  9. Let them cool for 10 min
